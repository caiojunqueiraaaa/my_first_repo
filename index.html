The San Antonio Spurs

The Spurs is a franchise of the National Basketball Association, part of the Western Conference in Texas, and are among the most popular teams in the league Won their first title in 1999 and managed to win the League 5 times, in 2003/2005/2007/2014. The team achieved the taboo of staying for 20 years in the playoffs, with the same coach Greg Popovich. Greg was also in charge of the US basketball team until the year 2021..

One of the greatest players in the franchise was Tim Duncan, who along with David Robinson formed the Twin Towers. Duncan is 2.13 and Robinson is 2.16 tall.

For many years, the franchise has had a history of signing many foreign players.Tim Duncan was born in the Vergens Islands, but holds North American nationality. The team also had the Argentine star Manu Ginobili and the Frenchman Tony Parker, who formed a trio with a lot of potential and were part of 4 titles, out of 5, for Spurs. San Antonio's last title, in 2014, the team beat the strong Miami Heat of Lebron James and Dwayne Wade, 4 games to 1.
