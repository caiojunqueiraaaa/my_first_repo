San Antonio Spurs reveal their special kit for season 50

PLAYERS WEARING THE NEW UNIFORM DURING THE 2022-23 NBA SEASON

The San Antonio Spurs have unveiled their special uniform for the 2022-23 NBA season, the season in which the team celebrates 50 years in the biggest basketball league on the planet. The “Spurs NBA Classic Edition 2022-23” honors the team’s 50-year legacy and brings back the iconic jersey worn by Spurs legend George “The Iceman” Gervin when the team played at Hemisfair Arena. boldly bears the “San Antonio” branding on the chest of her black shirt. This long-awaited kit tells the story of the team's arrival in former San Antonio and its unwavering fans who carried the Silver and Black tradition from one generation to the next.

In a press conference, San Antonio Spurs vice president said: "The Spurs Classic Edition kit is a symbolic way for us to honor our legacy, players and loyalty to the city of San Antonio, while also giving our fans what they're asking - we hear you. Our team has been working for two years to make this jersey possible on our 50th anniversary and we hope our fans are as excited as we are to see it on the court once again."

UNIFORM HISTORY

The nostalgic black-on-black uniform is part of the team's 2022-23 season celebration to commemorate the 50th anniversary of Spurs basketball in San Antonio, which began in 1973 and was worn by the franchise's first basketball players.

The Spurs Classic Edition kits feature "San Antonio" branding front and center on the shirtfront, player numbers are sewn onto the front and back of the shirt in a classic font with the sepia-toned NBA logo in the center from the back of the shirt. Both sides of the jersey's shorts feature a retroactive diamond design with the team logo from their Hemisfair days during the late '70s and early '80s.

The Spurs will wear their new kits in select home and away games during the 2022-23 season. The jersey, which must be one of the most desired of the season, will be available in September at the NBA store in the United States. The complete collection will feature t-shirts, hoodies, jackets, tees, caps and more.
